    We make unique cases using blockchain technology, we know... we are awesome!

    Blockcase was founded by three master students from Politecnico di Milano. In an attempt to express themselves creatively, they thought about making a product that could represent them. The values in mind were; ELEGANT, TECH-DRIVEN & EXCLUSIVE. After giving it a thought, they manage to create Blockcases, the FIRST iphone case company that has as head designer the blockchain technology. Each iphone case design is unique due to the fact that is linked to a specific hash. Our collections are based on cryptocurrencies and we strive to be leaders in the Blockchain. Still not convinced? Check out our shop to see the exclusive designs that are been generated.
